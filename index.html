Building Simulation and Optimization 2020

September 21st and 22nd 2020

IBPSA England's First Virtual Conference

Hosted by the Building Energy Research Group at Loughborough University

Sponsored by

This conference will set out, debate and address the challenges that we, the Building Simulation and Optimization community, are currently facing. Understanding and modelling the performance of built environment has never been more critical as we transform to a resource efficient and zero carbon future. Topics to be explored include

Urban building energy modelling

Energy flexibility

Design optimization

Data-driven modelling

Occupant behaviour

Health and Indoor Air Quality

Uncertainity Modelling

Control Systems

Building Systems Simulation

Daylight and Climate Modelling

Advanced Modelling Techniques

What is a Virtual Conference?

A virtual conference is a curated series of webinars, online meet-ups, and one-to-one chats to enable the exchange of knowledge, creation of new networks and generation of innovative ideas.

Benefits of a Virtual Conference

Recorded Presentations available to conference attendees

Broaden Your Reach. Attendees can participate from all over the globe.

Connect with more people through facilitated networking

Reduce greenhouse gas emissions from reduced air travel

 

Authors Instructions

The full papers should be between 6 and 8 pages long. The abstract should include the papers objectives, research methods, and novelty. More details can be found in either the word or Latex templates.
